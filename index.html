River Conservation

Ever since the first civilizations sprung up on their banks, major rivers have continued to be the lifeline of human society across the globe. The increasing pressures of a burgeoning human population and haphazard development have played havoc with the riverine ecosystems. Herein lies the genesis of this project on 'River Conservation'.

Rudrabhishek Enterprises Pvt Ltd (REPL), which was working with the State Government of Madhya Pradesh on the 'Narmada River Conservation' project, engaged Posidiff Advisor Private Limited to conduct a study on the issues involved in sustainable river conservation. Tracing the history of human civilization in terms of rivers through ancient, medieval and modern times, the study examines various phases of the Ganga Action Plan and Yamuna Action Plan, and their impact on rejuvenating the heartline of India. Even as it gives a brief overview of the various drainage systems in India, it delves deeper into the rivers Narmada, Sabarmati and Gomti.

The study comes out with a number of recommendations to take forward sustainable river development and conservation. True to its name, Posidiff has been positive and constructive in its approach.
